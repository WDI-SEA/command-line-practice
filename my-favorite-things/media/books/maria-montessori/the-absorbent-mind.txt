The Absorbent Mind assertst that children take in information from the world around them from birth onward. Children are non-discrimninartory when it comes to learning, they take in every detail that they are presented with unconciously from the ages of birth to six. After this period learning extends the child's mind to accept abstractions about the world around them. While in the early years thier mind are developing the schemas they will use to define the world around them through hands on real-world experiences.
