Viggo Mortenson plays an eccentric hippie father who is determined to give his children a rugged upbringing. He shows his children how to live alongside mother nature and  raises them to be critical thinkers. Tragic events occur and he is forced to take his family into main-stream society where he faces the fact that he may be endangering his children with his extremist lifestyle choices. This has him reconsider his position of living in the wild. He chooses to find balance and moves his family to a farm where they live a self-sustaining lifestyle.
